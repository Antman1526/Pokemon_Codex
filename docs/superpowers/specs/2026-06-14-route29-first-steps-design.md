# Route 29 First Steps Design

## Decision

Route 29 becomes Johto's first real outdoor route: a calm road with hidden tension. It should not spike difficulty yet. Instead, it teaches that Johto is slower, older, and more observant than Kanto.

## Player Experience

Red appears as field support, not as a battle controller. He frames the route as Antman's road and confirms that gyms remain Antman's challenge. Lyra points toward Cherrygrove and explains that Johto's guide network notices travelers quickly. A Silver shadow appears without a battle, keeping him mysterious until Cherrygrove or Route 30. The route also introduces day and night as a real encounter concept.

## Scope

- Add playable `MAP_ROUTE29` using Route 1's safe FRLG layout.
- Connect New Bark north to Route 29 and Route 29 south to New Bark.
- Set `FLAG_ROUTE29_FIRST_STEPS_REACHED`.
- Add Red field support, Lyra Cherrygrove handoff, Silver shadow, and day/night route sign text.
- Advance WorldLink from Route 29 to `cherrygrove_first_arrival`.

## Follow-Up

Cherrygrove should become the next slice. It should reward exploration and guidance rather than combat: Map Card, first route checklist upgrade, and the first stronger Silver clue.
