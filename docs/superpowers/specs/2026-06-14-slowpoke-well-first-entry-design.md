# Slowpoke Well First Entry Design

## Intent

Slowpoke Well is Johto's first real Rocket resurgence. It should feel smaller than Silph or Game Corner, but more personal: Rocket is hurting a town's protected Pokemon, Gold Dust is trying to convert the crime into a market, and Kurt becomes the emotional partner while Red deliberately holds the town exit.

## Story Shape

- Kurt enters the Well with Antman as the moral anchor. He does not fight for the player, but he frames the crisis as protection, not profit.
- Red stays outside Azalea and keeps the cave road clear. His absence inside the Well is intentional: this is Antman and Kurt's scene.
- Rocket is openly responsible for the immediate harm.
- Gold Dust quietly betrays Rocket by documenting the crime and preparing to sell "legal cleanup" after Rocket takes the blame.
- Silver watches from the Well-side shadow but still does not battle; the first post-Well confrontation should happen after the crisis breathes.
- Bugsy's Gym unlocks as the next required node after the Well is stabilized.

## Player-Facing Content

- Add `MAP_SLOWPOKE_WELL_B1F` as a Johto underground/indoor map reached from Azalea Town.
- Add an Azalea warp into Slowpoke Well and a Well warp back to Azalea.
- Add story touchpoints for Kurt, Rocket, Gold Dust, Slowpoke rescue, Red's outside watch, Silver trace, and Bugsy unlock.
- Add flags for first entry, Kurt partner scene, and Rocket evidence scene.
- Advance WorldLink from `slowpoke_well_first_entry` to `bugsy_gym_challenge`; Hoenn remains locked.

## Systems Notes

- The Well should introduce "story dungeon cleared, Gym unlocked" pacing for Johto.
- Apricorn Balls should start as a first-batch reward after the Well, then expand after Bugsy or Goldenrod.
- This slice does not add Bugsy's battle yet. The next milestone should add Gym arrival, Bugsy's swarm lesson, and post-Well rematch/QoL hooks.
