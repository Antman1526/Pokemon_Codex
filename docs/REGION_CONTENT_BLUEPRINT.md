# Pokemon Nexus Red - Region Content Blueprint

Date: 2026-06-13
Goal: every region gets a complete 10+ hour main-story chapter while staying feasible as a GBA game.

## 1. Region Scope Rule

All nine regions should be done. In practice, that means every region must have a full-feeling campaign arc, not a cameo. Each region needs:

- arrival hub,
- major travel route,
- all gym leaders or trial equivalents represented,
- regional villain conflict,
- regional rival thread,
- regional mechanic,
- legendary or mythic climax,
- side activities,
- exit hook.

GBA constraint: not every retail route/interior can be recreated tile-for-tile. The target is "complete adventure chapter," not "nine retail games copied into one ROM."

## 12. Completion Before Final Boss

Because all Pokemon must be catchable before the final boss:

- each region must unlock a meaningful chunk of the National Dex,
- late Paldea/World Championship must unlock final rare species,
- mythicals can use difficult pre-final quests,
- legendaries can be guardian trials before the final dungeon,
- postgame improves convenience and rematches, not base availability.
