# Pokemon Nexus Red - Red Companion System

Date: 2026-06-13

## 1. Role

Red is Antman's friend from Pallet Town and the living connection to the original Pokemon Red path. He is not the protagonist. He is the trainer Antman grew up hearing about, then actually gets to travel with.

Red should feel special without stealing the game.

Design rule: Red helps at key moments, but Antman solves the journey.

## 2. Early Kanto Use

Red appears before Oak's Lab or shortly after the starter scene.

Opening idea:

- Antman exits home.
- Red is waiting near the Pallet grass line, watching unusual Pokemon movement.
- Red says little but confirms Oak is worried.
- Blue is irritated because Red is paying attention to Antman.
- Red does not take a starter from Oak; he already has Pikachu or a compact early team.

## 3. Companion States

Red should not follow the player for the entire game. Use companion states:

- present_in_town: Red appears for dialogue or a scene.
- follows_temporarily: Red walks with player through a short route/dungeon.
- tag_partner: Red joins a scripted double battle.
- worldlink_contact: Red sends a short message.
- absent_investigating: Red is offscreen advancing a parallel lead.
- superboss_available: postgame friend battle unlocked.

## 4. Kanto Companion Beats

### Pallet Town

Red notices the route migration before Oak explains it. He tells Antman, "Do not ignore small changes. They become big ones."

### Route 1

Optional short follow section. Red points out that a Johto starter should not be nesting near Pallet.

### Viridian Forest

Red appears if the player struggles or after a rival update. He does not solve the dungeon; he marks a hidden item or warns about a Rocket scout.

### Pewter Museum

Red can be the tag partner if Ava or Dax is not selected. His presence makes Rocket panic.

### Post-Brock

Red leaves to check Route 22 and Mt. Moon rumors, creating space for Antman to lead.

## 5. Red Team Philosophy

Red's team should grow slowly and be used sparingly.

Early:

- Pikachu
- Bulbasaur/Charmander/Squirtle rotating cameo only if needed
- Snorlax or Lapras should not appear early

Midgame:

- Pikachu
- Kanto ace rotation
- one Pokemon that reflects the current region

Late game:

- iconic Red-style full team,
- no gimmick spam unless postgame,
- extremely strong but fair.

## 6. Mechanical Uses

Red can enable:

- optional double battle tutorials,
- tag battle spectacle,
- emergency story rescue once only,
- lore contrast with Blue,
- postgame friend battle,
- WorldLink "Red's Notes" tips.

Red should not:

- heal the player constantly,
- block player choice,
- defeat bosses for the player,
- appear in every town,
- become an eleventh rival feed spammer.

## 7. Relationship With Blue

Blue resents that Red treats Antman like a peer. This gives Blue a cleaner emotional arc:

- early jealousy,
- midgame rivalry escalation,
- late respect,
- World Championship acceptance.

Blue should eventually admit that chasing Red made him miss what Antman was becoming.

## 8. Relationship With Antman

Antman starts as the new trainer following Red's path. By the final act, Red is following Antman's lead into the Nexus crisis.

Key emotional turn:

During the World Nexus Championship, Red says or implies: "This one is your road."
