# Pokemon Nexus Red - Full Pokemon Availability Strategy

Date: 2026-06-13
Decision: all Pokemon through Generation 9 must be catchable before the final boss.

## 1. Rule

The player should be able to complete the base National Dex before entering the final Meridian Engine battle. Postgame can make completion easier, add rematches, improve shiny hunting, and add alternate forms, but base species availability should not be locked behind credits.

## 2. Availability Channels

Use many acquisition channels so every Pokemon does not have to be stuffed into grass tables.

- standard wild encounters,
- time-of-day encounters,
- weather encounters,
- fishing rods,
- surfing/diving encounters,
- static overworld encounters,
- gifts,
- eggs,
- fossils,
- trades,
- swarms,
- outbreaks,
- DexNav-style chains if feasible,
- raid dens,
- Ultra Wormholes,
- Area Zero anomalies,
- Team Phoenix restoration,
- Team Gold Dust auctions,
- WorldLink rival tips,
- legendary guardian trials.

## 3. Region Unlock Philosophy

Each region should unlock Pokemon that fit its identity, plus a controlled number of cross-region anomalies.

- Kanto: early classics, all starters at rare rates, special starter foundation.
- Johto: baby Pokemon, friendship evolutions, night encounters, roaming legends.
- Hoenn: weather, water, fossils, tropical species, diving.
- Sinnoh/Hisui: evolutions, mythic/ancient forms, mountain/cold species.
- Unova: urban/rural split, high species density, ideology-linked gifts.
- Kalos: Fairy type, Mega-linked species, beauty/style rewards.
- Alola: regional variants, island species, Ultra Beasts.
- Galar: raid species, fossils, competitive rewards, Dynamax-linked encounters.
- Paldea: open-world species, Titans, paradox/anomaly species, Tera shard hunts.
- World Nexus Championship pre-final: final missing species, mythicals, and legendary guardian access.

## 4. Legendary and Mythical Policy

Legendaries should be obtainable before final boss, but not casually.

Recommended structure:

- regional box legends: story guardian trials after each regional climax,
- roaming legends: Johto-style tracker or WorldLink sightings,
- Ultra Beasts: Alola wormhole hunts,
- paradox Pokemon: Paldea/Area Zero pre-final,
- mythicals: difficult optional quests unlocked before World Nexus Championship.

Do not require event distributions. The game should be self-contained.

## 5. Form Policy

Base species completion is required before final boss.

Forms:

- regional forms should be available in their relevant regions,
- battle forms should be handled through items/abilities where possible,
- cosmetic/forms with huge asset burden can be phased,
- shiny availability exists from the start but is improved by late-game tools.

## 6. Anti-Overload Encounter Rule

Do not place too many rare Pokemon in one route table. If a route has more than 8-12 desirable rare targets, split them by:

- morning/day/night,
- weather,
- fishing/surfing,
- overworld static,
- swarm,
- WorldLink hint,
- post-badge table upgrade.

## 7. Completion Before Final Boss

Before the final dungeon opens, WorldLink should offer a "Pokedex Readiness" check:

- missing species by region,
- known availability hints,
- current swarms/outbreaks,
- remaining legendary guardian trials,
- remaining fossil/restoration targets,
- remaining trade/evolution item needs.

This should guide completion without forcing it.
